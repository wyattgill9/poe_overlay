Make sure to fill out your resist, you want 75% fire/cold/lightning resist. Chaos resist is good, but not mandatory to have 75%, I usually aim for 30-50%.

Rings/glove: try to focus on high flat damage, preferable lightning/phys.

Weapon: You want the highest dps ele/phys weapon you can afford, preferable on a Gemini Bow base.
For bow sockets you default with Greater Stone Rune, but if your bow has flat phys damage than Greater Iron Rune will provide more dps.

Quiver: Just good dps stats, high flat/bow dmg or projectile speed with attack speed. Ideally just aim for 3-4 stats from the quiver I showed.

amulet: Preferable want +proj levels, besides that aim for life/resist. If you get 30 spirit you can swap Summon Companion for Rhoa Mount

Chest: you want the highest evasion chest you can afford.

Rarity is still really good in 0.3, I would try to hit at least 100% if you want to farm efficiently (don't focus on this if you feel like you're lacking damage or defense)

Jewels on tree: Just looking for generic damage: attack damage, elemental damage, movement speed, bonus from quivers, projectile damage, attack speed, etc

important:
You'll want 1 sapphire jewel with mana on kill to sustain mana during mapping.
If you want to completely solve mana, you can take Walker of the Wilds and get mana leech on ring or gloves.

How it Plays

Mapping gameplay/Rotation:
Only press Lightning Arrow, if your damage is weak than you might need to press Lightning Rod on tanky rares. For map bosses you'll also use Tornado Shot and Barrage on Lightning Arrow

How it Works

Quest rewards to take:

1) charm recharge - act 2
2) stun threshold from venom reward - act 3
3) tattoos - lightning resist/int/fire resist - act 4
4) boon - movement speed - interlude

Deadeye Ascendancy points:

In order: Gathering winds > Point blank > Endless munitions > windward

Gear Progression:

1) Fully setup non-crit setup, I would prio a headhunter before swapping to crit
(have heart of the well, cheap for a lot of extra damage/3 prop jewels).
2) Focus on a really good Crit bow And crit quiver before swapping to crit
3) Big evasion or ES/Evasion chest is massive for defense
4) Aim for a 450~ ES helmet when swapping to hybrid
5) After that I would focus on Solid boots/gloves/rings/amulet (+2 proj/spirit)
6) Get against the darkness with as extra lightning (eventually double as extra).
7) From here you can either go for the 50% MS boots or more damage (From Nothing)
8) Attack speed gloves are really solid for the price as well, would get these after the boots.

Low priority (too expensive to justify the power they give at the moment, buy if you have currency).
Rakiata's Flow/Dialla's Desire/Rite of Passage
These are solid upgrades, but not priority over the above.
